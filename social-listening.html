Project Overview

This project applies social media listening techniques to Sephora product review data, comparing sentiment profiles for two luxury skincare brands: L'Occitane and La Mer. Using VADER sentiment scoring, word cloud visualization, and part-of-speech tagging, I quantify which brand holds stronger customer sentiment and identify key drivers of satisfaction and dissatisfaction.

Analysis Plan

  • 01Exploratory data analysis — rating distributions, review volume, mean ratings by brand
  • 02Review length analysis — histogram of character counts, long vs. short review segmentation
  • 03VADER sentiment scoring — compound scores by star rating, statistical significance testing
  • 04Word cloud generation — high vs. low rating reviews, brand comparison
  • 05Part-of-speech tagging — adjective extraction for satisfied vs. dissatisfied reviewers
  • 06Social media strategy — content recommendations based on Dragonfly and STEPPS frameworks
